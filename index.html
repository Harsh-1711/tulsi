about us

Reviving the adaptogenic consciousness of tulsi in modern India involves reintroducing its traditional healing properties and cultural significance, fostering a renewed appreciation for its holistic benefits in promoting well-being and resilience. Through education, research, and public awareness campaigns, we aim to reconnect individuals with the sacred heritage of tulsi as a revered adaptogen deeply rooted in Indian culture and tradition.
